Welcome to YardGuardAI

Automated wildlife detection for the modern world!

Overview

YardGuardAI utilizes state-of-the-art computer vision and sound technology to monitor your environment for wildlife. Continuously operational, it seamlessly detects and identifies various animals in real-time.

Features

  • Real-time Motion Detection: Instantly captures movement in your surroundings.
  • AI-Driven Animal Identification: Utilizes advanced algorithms to recognize and classify animals.
  • Dynamic Sound Playback: Plays specific sounds or frequencies tailored to the identified animal.
  • Customizable Sound Files: Easily add or modify sound triggers for different animals.
  • Next-Gen User Interface: Experience a sleek and intuitive GUI for user engagement.

How It Works

  1. Setup: Connect your webcam and initiate the application.
  2. Continuous Monitoring: The system tracks video frames and identifies motion.
  3. Intelligent Analysis: Captured frames are analyzed via AI for animal identification.
  4. Interactive Sound Response: Sounds are activated based on detected animals.
  5. Historic Logging: Images and events are logged for future reference.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of animals can YardGuardAI detect?

Our AI can recognize a wide range of animals, including deer, raccoons, and birds, among others.

What hardware is necessary?

A simple webcam and a sound system are required for optimal performance.

Can I customize the sound files played?

Absolutely! You can easily replace or add new sound files in the sound_files directory.

Is there a user manual available?

While there isn't a formal user manual yet, feel free to reach out for support.

Stay tuned for updates as we continuously improve the app with AI advancements and additional features!
